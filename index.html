The training gym for
Lean AI.

Phasor connects data-efficient model developers with neuromorphic, analog, and asynchronous chip makers — providing proprietary training data and pipelines for developers, and benchmark diagnostics for hardware makers. Two sides, one platform, one critical abstraction layer.

Neuromorphic Data Platform

The largest database of neuromorphic data — and growing. Proprietary event-based data converted from visual sources, with audio and gesture pipelines next. Accessible to pilot research groups today, with a Mag 7 company and a European car manufacturer in confirmation for their neuromorphic units.
